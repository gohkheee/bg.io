BOTANIC GARDENS

Singapore's first UNESCO world heritage awarded location!
Perfect place to enjoy the the Garden City at its peak! Enjoyable for all ages, spend quality time with your family and friends here while surrounded by nature's beauty.


History

Built in 1859, Singapore's Botanic Gardens is the oldest garden found on the island.
In its early years, Botanic Gardens served as Singapore's scientific garden, allowing the nation to study their local flora and fauna so that they can be preserved today.
It also aided the island in experimenting, developing and distributing potentially useful plants to help Singapore grow.
Today, Botanic Gardens serve as a precious historical site as well as a beautiful place for al to enjoy.
